Free fuse bead pattern generator — turn any photo into a Perler, Hama or Artkal pattern

Fuse Bead Pattern Maker converts photos, illustrations and pixel art into fuse bead patterns automatically. Unlike generic pixelators, it quantizes your image to colors that are actually sold — Perler, Hama (midi/mini), Artkal (5mm and 2.6mm) and Kawada Nanobeads — so the pattern doubles as a shopping list. Pixel art is auto-detected and reproduced dot-for-dot (one pixel = one bead), including AI-generated pixel art with a broken grid.

The tool tallies exactly which bead colors and how many of each you need, with purchase links per color, and exports the pattern as a PNG with grid coordinates and color symbols. Large designs get pegboard split lines. Free, no sign-up, no install — and your images are processed entirely in your browser, never uploaded. See the guide & FAQ for details.
